Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. For annual plans, we also accept bank transfers.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Starter and Professional plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. Enterprise plans can request a custom demo.

What happens if I exceed my monthly search limit?

If you exceed your monthly search limit, you'll have the option to purchase additional searches or upgrade your plan. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status to receive this discount.

Need a Custom Solution?

Our Enterprise plan is tailored for large organizations with specific needs.
