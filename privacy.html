Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities at ConnectIndia

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how ConnectIndia Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile information and bio
  • Social media handles and links
  • Company information (for businesses)
  • Payment and billing information

Usage Information

  • Platform activity and interactions
  • Campaign performance data
  • Device information and IP address
  • Cookies and similar technologies

Content Information

  • Portfolio uploads and samples
  • Messages and communications
  • Campaign deliverables and content
  • Reviews and ratings

2. How We Use Your Information

Platform Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate connections between influencers and businesses
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Provide customer support

Improvement & Analytics

  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Improve our services and features
  • Generate insights and recommendations
  • Conduct research and development

Communication

  • Send important platform updates
  • Notify about new opportunities
  • Marketing communications (with consent)
  • Security and fraud prevention

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

  • Profile information visible to potential collaborators
  • Campaign-related communications
  • Public portfolio and work samples

Service Providers

  • Payment processing partners
  • Cloud storage and hosting providers
  • Analytics and marketing tools
  • Customer support platforms

Legal Requirements

  • Compliance with applicable laws
  • Response to legal requests
  • Protection of rights and safety
  • Fraud prevention and security

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations and authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Compliance: SOC 2 and industry standard compliance
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

5. Your Rights and Choices

Account Control

  • Update your profile and preferences
  • Control visibility of your information
  • Manage communication preferences
  • Delete your account at any time

Data Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Data portability and export
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

8. Children's Privacy

ConnectIndia is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on our platform
  • Update to the "Last Updated" date
