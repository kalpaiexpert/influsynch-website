Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions

How do I get started as an influencer?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Sign up with your email and create your profile
  2. Add your social media links and portfolio
  3. Complete verification to build trust
  4. Browse opportunities or wait for brand invitations
  5. Start collaborating and earning!
How do brands find influencers on the platform?

Brands can discover influencers through:

  • Advanced search filters (niche, location, follower count)
  • AI-powered recommendations based on campaign goals
  • Browse by categories and content types
  • View verified profiles with performance metrics
How are payments handled on ConnectIndia?

Our secure payment system includes:

  • Escrow system that protects both parties
  • Payments released upon deliverable approval
  • Support for UPI, bank transfers, and digital wallets
  • Automated invoicing and tax documentation
  • 24-48 hour payment processing
What are the platform fees?

Our transparent fee structure:

  • Free for influencers to join and create profiles
  • 15% platform fee on completed collaborations
  • Monthly subscription plans for businesses starting at ‚Çπ9,999
  • No hidden fees or surprise charges
  • Enterprise solutions with custom pricing
How long does the verification process take?

Verification typically takes:

  • 24-48 hours for basic profile verification
  • Social media account verification within 24 hours
  • Business verification may take 2-3 business days
  • Priority verification available for premium accounts
  • Email notifications sent for each step
What support is available if I face issues?

We offer comprehensive support:

  • 24/7 chat support for urgent issues
  • Email support with 4-hour response time
  • Video tutorials and knowledge base
  • Dedicated account managers for enterprise clients
  • Community forums and peer support
