Our Story

Born from the understanding that India's diverse digital landscape needed a platform that truly understands local culture, languages, and business dynamics.

Building India's Digital Bridge

Founded in 2023 in Mumbai, ConnectIndia emerged from a simple observation: while global platforms existed, none truly understood the nuances of Indian influencer marketing - from regional language preferences to cultural celebrations, from tier-2 city micro-influencers to Bollywood celebrities.

Our founders, having worked extensively in India's digital marketing space, recognized the gap between authentic Indian creators and businesses looking to connect with diverse audiences across the country's 1.4 billion population.

Today, we're proud to be India's fastest-growing influencer marketing platform, trusted by brands from startups to Fortune 500 companies, and creators from metros to tier-3 cities.

Why India Needed ConnectIndia

  • Regional language content creators were underrepresented
  • Tier-2 and tier-3 city influencers lacked opportunities
  • Businesses struggled with cultural authenticity
  • Limited understanding of Indian festivals and trends
  • Complex pricing structures unsuited for Indian market
