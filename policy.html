Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are our top priorities

Last Updated: January 15, 2024 | Effective Date: January 15, 2024

Introduction

ConnectIndia ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our influencer-business connection platform.

This policy applies to all users of ConnectIndia, including influencers, businesses, agencies, and visitors to our website. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Important: This Privacy Policy is compliant with the Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), Information Technology Act, 2000, and international standards including GDPR.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • • Name, email address, phone number
  • • Profile information and bio
  • • Business registration details
  • • Payment and billing information
  • • Government-issued ID for verification
  • • Tax identification numbers (PAN, GST)

Platform Data

  • • Social media profiles and metrics
  • • Campaign performance data
  • • Communication logs and messages
  • • Usage analytics and behavior
  • • Device and browser information
  • • IP address and location data

Third-Party Platform Data

When you connect your social media accounts (Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter), we access publicly available information and analytics data in accordance with each platform's API terms and your authorization.

How We Use Your Information

Platform Services

Facilitate connections between influencers and businesses, manage campaigns, and process payments

Analytics & Insights

Provide performance analytics, audience insights, and campaign optimization recommendations

Security & Compliance

Verify identities, prevent fraud, ensure platform security, and comply with legal requirements

Specific Use Cases:

  • • Creating and managing user accounts and profiles
  • • Matching influencers with relevant business opportunities
  • • Processing payments and maintaining financial records
  • • Providing customer support and resolving disputes
  • • Sending important notifications and updates
  • • Improving our services through data analysis
  • • Complying with tax reporting and legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

When We Share Information

  • • With Platform Users: Profile information visible to potential collaborators
  • • Service Providers: Payment processors, analytics tools, and infrastructure providers
  • • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court orders, or government requests
  • • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • • With Consent: When you explicitly authorize specific sharing

We Never Share

  • • Your personal contact information without permission
  • • Private messages or communications
  • • Payment details or financial information
  • • Information for marketing purposes to third parties
  • • Data with competitors or unauthorized entities

Data Security

Technical Safeguards

256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
Secure cloud infrastructure with regular backups
Multi-factor authentication for sensitive accounts
Continuous monitoring and threat detection

Operational Safeguards

Limited access on need-to-know basis
Regular security training for all staff
Annual security audits and penetration testing
Incident response and breach notification procedures

Your Privacy Rights

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data (with certain limitations)

Right to Object

Object to processing for marketing or legitimate interests

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your personal information

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@connectindia.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

We may require identity verification before processing certain requests to protect your privacy and security.
