Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining ConnectIndia as a creator.

How do I get started as a creator?

Simply sign up for free, complete your profile with your social media accounts and content samples, and wait for verification. Once verified, you can start applying to campaigns immediately.

What are the minimum requirements?

We welcome creators of all sizes! Minimum requirements: 1,000+ followers on at least one platform, original content creation, and an active Indian audience. Quality engagement matters more than follower count.

How and when do I get paid?

Payments are processed within 7 days of campaign completion via UPI or bank transfer. All payments are protected and guaranteed through our platform.

Can I work with brands outside the platform?

Yes, you're free to work independently. We only facilitate collaborations for campaigns posted on our platform. You retain complete control over your other business activities.

Is there any fee to join?

Joining ConnectIndia is completely free for creators. We only take a small commission from successful collaborations to maintain the platform and provide support services.
